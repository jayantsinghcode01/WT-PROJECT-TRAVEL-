Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my tour?

We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for popular destinations like Everest Base Camp or during peak seasons. However, we can often accommodate last-minute bookings depending on availability.

Do you offer custom itineraries?

Absolutely! Our custom itinerary service is one of our specialties. We work with you to create a personalized adventure that matches your interests, fitness level, budget, and timeline.

What's included in your tour packages?

Our packages typically include accommodation, meals during the trek, expert guides, permits, and airport transfers. Specific inclusions vary by tour - check individual tour pages for detailed information.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

We understand plans can change. Our cancellation policy varies by tour and timing. Generally, cancellations 60+ days before departure receive full refunds minus processing fees. Contact us for specific details.

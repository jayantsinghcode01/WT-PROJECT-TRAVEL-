Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours, booking process, and adventure travel

How far in advance should I book my tour? +

We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for popular destinations like Everest Base Camp, especially during peak seasons (March-May and September-November). For less popular routes or off-season travel, 4-6 weeks is usually sufficient. However, we can often accommodate last-minute bookings depending on availability.

Early booking advantages include:

  • Better accommodation options
  • Preferred departure dates
  • Early bird discounts (up to $200 off)
  • More time for physical preparation

What is your cancellation policy? +

Our cancellation policy varies by tour and timing:

  • 60+ days before departure: Full refund minus $100 processing fee
  • 30-59 days: 75% refund
  • 15-29 days: 50% refund
  • Less than 15 days: 25% refund

We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances. Medical emergencies and natural disasters may qualify for different terms.

Can I book a tour for just myself (solo traveler)? +

Absolutely! We welcome solo travelers and have many single adventurers join our group tours. Solo travelers often find our tours a great way to meet like-minded people and make lasting friendships.

Solo traveler considerations:

  • Single room supplements may apply
  • Option to share accommodation to reduce costs
  • Private guide services available
  • Special support for first-time solo travelers

What's included in your tour packages? +

Our tour packages typically include:

  • Expert English-speaking guide
  • Accommodation as specified in itinerary
  • All meals during trekking portions
  • Airport transfers
  • Required permits and entrance fees
  • Porter services (where applicable)
  • Group medical kit

Not included: International flights, visa fees, travel insurance, personal equipment, tips, and meals in cities (unless specified).

Do you offer custom itineraries? +

Yes! Custom itinerary planning is one of our specialties. We work closely with you to create a personalized adventure that matches your interests, fitness level, budget, and timeline.

Custom tour process:

  • Initial consultation to understand your preferences
  • Detailed itinerary proposal with options
  • Revisions based on your feedback
  • Final booking and preparation support

Custom tours start at $150 planning fee, which is credited toward your final booking.

What is the maximum group size? +

We keep our groups small to ensure personalized attention and minimal environmental impact:

  • Trekking tours: Maximum 12 people
  • Safari tours: Maximum 8 people per vehicle
  • Cultural tours: Maximum 15 people
  • Beach adventures: Maximum 10 people

Smaller groups allow for more flexibility, better wildlife viewing opportunities, and stronger connections with local communities.

How do you ensure safety during adventures? +

Safety is our top priority. Our comprehensive safety measures include:

  • Certified guides with wilderness first aid training
  • Regular safety briefings and equipment checks
  • Emergency communication devices (satellite phones)
  • Comprehensive medical kits and emergency oxygen
  • Established evacuation procedures
  • 24/7 emergency support team
  • Regular weather monitoring and route adjustments

All guides undergo annual safety training and certification updates.

What happens if I get altitude sickness? +

Altitude sickness is a serious concern on high-altitude treks. Our prevention and response protocol includes:

  • Gradual ascent profiles with proper acclimatization days
  • Daily health checks by guides
  • Emergency oxygen and medical supplies
  • Immediate descent if symptoms worsen
  • Helicopter evacuation insurance coverage

Warning signs: Severe headache, nausea, confusion, difficulty walking. If you experience these symptoms, inform your guide immediately.

Is travel insurance required? +

Yes, comprehensive travel insurance is mandatory for all our adventure tours. Your insurance must cover:

  • Adventure sports and high-altitude activities
  • Emergency medical treatment and evacuation
  • Helicopter rescue (up to 6,000m for Himalayan treks)
  • Trip cancellation and interruption
  • Personal belongings and equipment

We can recommend suitable insurance providers or you can purchase through our partner for $125-250 depending on trip value and duration.

What documents do I need for international travel? +

Required documents vary by destination, but generally include:

  • Passport: Valid for at least 6 months beyond travel date
  • Visa: Required for most destinations (we provide guidance)
  • Permits: Special permits for restricted areas (we handle these)
  • Vaccinations: Yellow fever, hepatitis, etc. as required
  • Travel insurance: Proof of coverage required

We provide detailed document checklists 60 days before departure and assist with permit applications.

What vaccinations do I need? +

Vaccination requirements depend on your destination and personal medical history. Common recommendations include:

  • Routine vaccines: MMR, DPT, flu, COVID-19
  • Hepatitis A & B: Recommended for most destinations
  • Typhoid: For areas with poor sanitation
  • Japanese Encephalitis: For rural Asia travel
  • Yellow Fever: Required for some African destinations

Consult a travel medicine specialist 4-6 weeks before departure. We provide destination-specific health information with your booking confirmation.

What should I pack for my adventure? +

We provide detailed packing lists specific to each tour, but essential items include:

  • Clothing: Layering system, waterproof jacket, warm layers
  • Footwear: Sturdy hiking boots, comfortable camp shoes
  • Accessories: Sun hat, warm hat, gloves, sunglasses
  • Personal items: Sunscreen, first aid supplies, medications
  • Electronics: Camera, power bank, headlamp

Equipment rental is available in most destinations. We'll send a comprehensive packing list 45 days before departure.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express
  • Bank Transfer: Wire transfer for large amounts
  • PayPal: Secure online payments
  • Check: Personal or cashier's check (US only)

Payment schedule: 30% deposit to secure booking, balance due 45 days before departure. Payment plans available for tours over $3,000.

Do you offer payment plans? +

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans for tours over $3,000:

  • 3-month plan: 30% deposit, 35% at 60 days, 35% at 30 days
  • 6-month plan: 25% deposit, then 25% monthly for 3 months
  • Custom plans: Available for early bookings (6+ months)

No interest charges for payment plans. Final payment must be completed 30 days before departure. Contact us to set up a payment plan.

Are there any hidden fees? +

No hidden fees! Our pricing is transparent and includes all advertised services. The only additional costs are:

  • Optional services: Single room supplements, equipment rental
  • Personal expenses: Souvenirs, extra meals, drinks
  • Tips: For guides and porters (recommended amounts provided)
  • Travel insurance: If not already covered

All potential additional costs are clearly outlined in your booking confirmation and pre-departure information.

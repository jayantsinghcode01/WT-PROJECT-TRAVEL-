Tour Overview

Embark on the adventure of a lifetime with our 14-day Himalayan Trek, designed to showcase the incredible beauty and culture of Nepal's mountain regions. This expertly crafted journey takes you through diverse landscapes, from lush rhododendron forests to high-altitude glacial valleys.

Experience authentic Sherpa hospitality in traditional mountain villages, witness breathtaking sunrise views over the world's highest peaks, and challenge yourself on well-established trekking routes that have inspired adventurers for generations.

Our experienced guides ensure your safety while sharing deep knowledge of local culture, wildlife, and mountain ecology. This trek is perfect for adventurous travelers seeking an authentic Himalayan experience with proper acclimatization and support.

Fitness Requirements

This trek requires good physical fitness and prior hiking experience. Participants should be able to walk 5-7 hours per day on steep, rocky terrain at high altitude.

Recommended preparation: 3+ months of cardiovascular training, hiking practice with a backpack, and altitude training if possible.

14-Day Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

Altitude: 1,400m | Accommodation: Hotel

Arrive at Tribhuvan International Airport. Transfer to hotel. Orientation meeting and gear check. Welcome dinner with traditional Nepali cuisine.

Day 2: Fly to Lukla, Trek to Phakding

Altitude: 2,610m | Trekking: 3-4 hours | Accommodation: Tea house

Early morning flight to Lukla (35 minutes). Begin trek along the Dudh Koshi River. Cross several suspension bridges to reach Phakding village.

Day 3: Trek to Namche Bazaar

Altitude: 3,440m | Trekking: 5-6 hours | Accommodation: Tea house

Cross the famous Hillary Suspension Bridge. Steep ascent through pine forests. Enter Sagarmatha National Park. First views of Mount Everest.

Day 4: Acclimatization in Namche Bazaar

Altitude: 3,440m | Activities: Exploration | Accommodation: Tea house

Rest day for acclimatization. Visit Sherpa Museum and Hillary School. Optional hike to Hotel Everest View for panoramic mountain vistas.

Day 5: Trek to Tengboche

Altitude: 3,860m | Trekking: 5-6 hours | Accommodation: Tea house

Descend through rhododendron forests. Cross the Dudh Koshi River and ascend to Tengboche Monastery with stunning Everest views.

Days 6-12: High Altitude Trekking

Max Altitude: 5,500m | Various accommodations

Continue through Dingboche, Lobuche, and reach Everest Base Camp. Experience sunrise from Kala Patthar viewpoint. Gradual descent through beautiful mountain villages.

Day 13: Fly back to Kathmandu

Flight from Lukla | Accommodation: Hotel

Morning flight back to Kathmandu. Rest and recover. Celebration dinner with the team. Free time for shopping and sightseeing.

Day 14: Departure

Transfer to airport

Transfer to Tribhuvan International Airport for onward journey. Trek completion certificates presented.
